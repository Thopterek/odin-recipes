Mmm pancakes

Description

Its a standard pancake recipe, just like this text here: you just need to feel it

So remember don't worry, fail and do another pancake

  • First grab the ingredients

    • Milk, best 3%+
    • Eggs, 3 is best
    • Flour 450 (and alike)
    • Oil or butter
    • Sugar & Salt

  • Now lets grab cauldron and pan

    1. Put all whole legs into cauldron
    2. Mix them until foamy and then add a bit of sugar
    3. Add 1/4 or half of the flour (depending on appetite)
    4. Add whole milk and mix it up, if doe is too sticky add water
    5. Flowy but not too watery consistency and heat the pan
    6. A little bit of oil into the doe and on the pan
    7. Keep on one side untill fully yellow
    8. and short moment for the other side
  • Enjoy your pancakes!
