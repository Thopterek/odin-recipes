Uuu chicken soup

Another description

The chicken soup is the perfect soup for those in need

You might need more free time or you are sick, thats for you

Ingridients

  • Chicken, literally any
  • Noodles, literally any, 125g or less
  • Vegetables, you guessed it, literally any
  • Species, think about it... yes you are right!

Recipe after knowing all that

  1. Grab chicken and cut it up or put it as a whole thing
  2. after it boils and gets foamy (30 min), throw away the foam
  3. Add the vegetables and species
  4. Refill the water to be full
  5. Leave it for 2/3 hours
  6. in last 10/15 minutes add the noodles
Enjoy your soup!
